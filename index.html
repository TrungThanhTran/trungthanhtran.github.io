Thanks for stopping by!


I am Xinya Du (pronounced as shinn-ya do), I am working as a Postdoctoral Research Associate with Prof. Heng Ji at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Before, I earned a CS Ph.D. degree from Cornell University in 2021, advised by Prof. Claire Cardie. I graduated with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.


I enjoy exploring/building things that are novel and impactful (in research and life). I work on natural language processing and machine learning, with a current focus on context-aware methods for question asking & answering, and event extraction, and the interplay between them.


You can find more information about me in the Curriculum Vitae.
